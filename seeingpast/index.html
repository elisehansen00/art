Seeing Past

Project Goals

Using a cohesive series of ten images, I wanted to illustrate my changing relationship to my personal past and the history of the town I grew up in. I created a these images, printed on Kentmere Lustre darkroom paper, by mixing in-camera double exposures on 120mm film and simulating a double exposure effect by using important items to me as photograms overlaid on 35mm darkroom prints. This project was integral to developing my skills of darkroom printing and executing a project that mixed printing techniques with my personal storytelling abilities.
